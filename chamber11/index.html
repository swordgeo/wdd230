Local Business Shuts Doors After Lawsuit

Last Thursday it was announced by Arthur "Skeeter" Camwell, owner and proprietor of HotWings LLC, that the restaurant will be forced to close its doors due to lack accrued debts and lack of funding. This news comes mere days after the lawsuit against HotWings versus a former customer, Bernadine Bucket, who decided to take legal action against the restaurant following damages after a slip and fall apparently caused by fried chicken skins. Camwell and HotWings have been members of the Davenport Chamber of Commerce since 2013.
