Temple Inn & Suites

Worship Without Worry

"The temple is a point of intersection between heaven and earth. In this sacred place, holy work will be performed through selfless service and love. The temple reminds me of all that is good and beautiful in the world."

~ David A. Bednar

The temples of the Church are magnificient, beautiful buildings sanctified by our Heavenly Father to provide essential ordinances to church members and their families. Unfortunately, many find it difficult to travel the great distance to temples that in some parts of the world are few and far between.

The vision of Temple Inn & Suites is to provide the best possible accommodations for the most reasonable prices - whether your goal is to spend a day to perform sealings and baptisms, or even to stay for several months as a Temple Worker. We do this by being conveniently placed closeby temple grounds to provide a short commute.
