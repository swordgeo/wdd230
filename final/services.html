Temple History Sitting Area

Our main lobby and reception area boasts several adjoining sitting rooms that not only provide ample space for socializing and get-togethers outside your rooms, they also have interesting factoids about dozens of the temples built by the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. You can also see the locations of every temple serviced by Temple Inn & Suites in the present, as well as upcoming future developments!

Changing Rooms

We provide free access to changing rooms and bathroom facilities for temple patrons who have traveled far and need to change to and from church clothes. Available to both guests and the general public!

Family Search Center

Our state-of-the-art Family Search Center is available for free to the general public, and is fully equipped to high-speed internet that supports family search and ordinance work.

The computers of our search center are also available to all our patrons that wish to email, browse news, conduct business, or anything else they might desire.

History Consultation

Our family search center is staffed by a cadre of wonderful volunteers, both from the local church wards, as well as genealogy enthusiasts outside of the church that are eager to assist in all your family history queries.

Child Services

We provide the following services to the children of patrons.

Playroom

Our playroom is well stocked with toys, books, and games especially suitable for younger children.

Babysitting

Short-term babysitting services are also available for a small fee. Calling two or more weeks before your stay will ensure adequate scheduling!

Free Amenities

The following services are also freely available to all guests of Temple Inn & Suites at no extra charge.

  • Daily Continental Breakfast
  • Exercise and Workout Room
  • Indoor swimming pool
  • WiFi high-speed internet access and smart TVs
  • Temple shuttles
  • Parking

Wedding Reception Hall

For families that wish to get sealed in the temple and stay in the area for their wedding reception, consider reserving our wedding reception hall, which can provide seating up to 100 individuals. We also offer several options for catering and decoration at your request.

Temple Missionary Suites

For full-time temple missionaries, Temple Inn & Suites has a number of apartment-style kitchenette suites designed for long-term comfort and discounted pricing.
