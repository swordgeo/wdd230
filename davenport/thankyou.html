Thank you for joining the Chamber of Commerce of Davenport, Indiana!

As our own local resident Cletus Hogsun once said about the people of Davenport, and indeed, the people of the world:

"Folk [aren't able to do] nothing truly grand with only they own hands. Every great man what ever claimed to build his fortune by only [himself, I] reckon you'll find behind that man, if not the toil and sweat from [the individuals that work] under him, surely from the toil and sweat from his mama, his school, an' the Good Lord what gave him the gifts to do what he done."
