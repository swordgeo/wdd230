Weeny Haus

Believe it or not, Davenport is the sister city to Frankfurt, Germany! The employees of Weenie Haus show their respect every day by wearing these full-body costumes - especially in the kitchen!

Cletus Hogsun

A farmer and philospher of sorts, Cletus is often sought after for advice by locals and tourists who try to decipher the cryptic wisdom that's given him the unofficial title "Sage of the Corn Fields".

Couchville Estates Trailer Park

Davenport is well-known for having the most affordable housing developments in the congressional district. Neighborhoods like Couchville Estates facilitate a low cost-of-living and close-knit communities.

Goodbye HotWings - Who's Next?

For the last eight years the deliciously fried food from HotWings LLC has taken the tastebuds of Davenport by storm. Now that the location for what was formerly the most profitable restaurant in the city is up for grabs, which intrepid investors or aspiring chefs will claim it next?

Expressive Artwork

Davenport is a city with a long tradition of non-traditional artwork. Once you enter downtown (or even some resedential areas!) it's only a matter of time before you spot the handiwork from several of Davenport's self-taught artists!

Local Eateries

While Davenport has its share of national fast-food chains like any city, it also boasts a cadre of plucky entrepreneural restaurants that save money on advertising and flashy atmosphere to direct their passion directly into tasty food at a great price!

Passionate Protest

The socially engaged citizens of Davenport enjoy the opportunity to come together in peaceful demonstration and give direct suggestions and feedback to the mayor's legistative decisions.
